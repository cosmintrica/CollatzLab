# Structure of record-breaking seeds in [1, 20000]

- Claim ID: COL-6397
- Direction: hypothesis-sandbox
- Status: idea

## Statement

Record-breaking TST seeds in [1, 20000] have mean Hamming density 0.624 (above random expectation ~0.5). High bit density may correlate with longer orbits. Additionally, 55% of TST records are ≡ 3 (mod 4).
